Strawberry Cheesecake

About the Strawberry Cheesecake Cookie

The Strawberry Cheesecake Cookie is a delightful twist on a classic favorite. With a rich, creamy cheesecake base and a burst of sweet, tangy strawberries, this cookie is a perfect blend of indulgence and freshness. Here's how we make our irresistible Strawberry Cheesecake Cookies:

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup chopped strawberries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/4 cup white chocolate chips

Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda.

3. In a larger bowl, beat together the butter, sugar, and cream cheese until smooth and creamy.

4. Add the egg and vanilla extract, and mix well until combined.

5. Gradually stir in the dry ingredients until just combined.
