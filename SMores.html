S'Mores Cake

About the S'Mores Cake

This S'Mores Cake is the perfect blend of rich chocolate, gooey marshmallows, and crunchy graham crackers, all in a delicious cake. Here’s how we make our mouthwatering S'Mores Cake:

Ingredients

  • 1 box of chocolate cake mix
  • 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar

Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a round cake pan.

2. Prepare the cake mix according to the package instructions, then stir in the graham cracker crumbs and chocolate chips.

3. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

4. Once the cake is done, top it with mini marshmallows and return it to the oven for 3-5 minutes to melt the marshmallows.

5. In a small bowl, whisk together heavy cream and powdered sugar, then drizzle the whipped cream over the cake before serving.
