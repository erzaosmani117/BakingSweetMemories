Vanilla Bean Cupcake

About the Vanilla Bean Cupcake

The Vanilla Bean Cupcake is a light, fluffy, and aromatic treat. Infused with real vanilla bean flavor, these cupcakes are perfect for any celebration or a simple dessert indulgence. Here’s how we make our irresistible Vanilla Bean Cupcakes:

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla bean paste
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.

3. In a larger bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy, then add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.

4. Add the vanilla bean paste and mix until incorporated, then alternate adding the dry ingredients and milk, starting and ending with the dry ingredients.

5. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners and bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Let them cool before serving!
