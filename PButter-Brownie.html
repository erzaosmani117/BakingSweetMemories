Penautbutter Brownie

About the Peanut Butter Brownies

These Peanut Butter Brownies combine the richness of chocolate with the creamy goodness of peanut butter. A perfect treat for peanut butter lovers! Here’s how we make our delicious Peanut Butter Brownies:

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar

Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x9-inch baking pan.

2. In a bowl, whisk together melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla until smooth.

3. Stir in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt until combined, then pour the batter into the prepared pan.

4. In a small bowl, mix peanut butter and powdered sugar until smooth, then dollop over the brownie batter. Use a knife to swirl the peanut butter into the brownies.

5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out with a few moist crumbs. Let them cool, then slice and enjoy!
