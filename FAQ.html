What is Baking Sweet Memories?

Baking Sweet Memories is your go-to place for all things sweet! We specialize in cookies, cakes, and other baked goods that bring joy to your life.

How can I place an order?

You can easily place an order through our website. Simply select your desired products, add them to the cart, and proceed to checkout!

What are the shipping options?

We offer a variety of shipping options, including standard, expedited, and express shipping. Shipping costs will vary based on your location and the size of your order.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to many international destinations. Please check our shipping policy for more details on international shipping availability.

Are your cookies gluten-free?

We offer gluten-free cookies as part of our menu. Be sure to check the specific product page for detailed ingredients and dietary information.

What categories of cookies do you have?

We have the classical ones, signatures cookies, seasonal specials and bite sized sweets
